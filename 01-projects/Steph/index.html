La Constelación

Designing for the Safety of Undocumented Immigrants

In a country unjustly founded by immigrants, the term "illegal" is unjust for undocumented people in the U.S. The nation's immigration system has made it almost impossible for people who are fleeing difficult situations or looking for better opportunities, to establish homes away from their origin countries. In East Boston and all over the nation, ICE arrests have been occuring more often and breaking the very sense that immigrants came in search for - safety. Despite East Boston declaring itself a "Sanctuary City," a simple policy has proven not to be enough in the protection of undocumented folks.

How can sanctuary cities establish safety for
undocumented immigrants through design?

In order to create a sense of safety, there must be safe places for individuals. Through the use of community ties with leaders and organizations, safe locations, a series of mappings and case studies, a network of safety can be created in East Boston. We have more agency than we think we do. Undocumented immigrant right should not be up to solely national government policy. We can do more - from Policymakers, to planners/designers, to even a community member - we can all do something more in the protection of undocumented folks. The goal of this project is two-fold: (1) to create awareness on the unseen boundaries placed on undocumented folks on the daily, and (2) to propose a framework and guide that could break down these barriers and create a safer environment and community for immigrants.

WHO IS INVOLVED?

Policy Makers

I am policy makers with jurisdiction in East Boston. I want to fairly represent the community and address their needs, So that I can create safety and wellbeing for all people of the community.

Sensitive Locations

I am places of sensitive locations for immigrants. I want to protect immigrants in times of emergency from ICE, So that I can assure a temporary safe zone for UndImm and their families.

Activists

I am activist groups in East Boston for immigrants, So that I can help positively impact the quality of life for immigrants despite legal status standing.

Undocumented Immigrants

I am undocumented immigrants of East Boston. I want to be protected from deporation or acquire regular status, So that I can feel safe in the home I've built in East Boston.

ICE

I am U.S. Immigration and Customes Enforcement. I want to protect America from cross-border crime and illegal immigration, So that I can create safety and wellbeing for people of legal status.

Designers

I am designers and planners. I want to plan and design a healthy and prospering city, So that all residents of East Boston can feel safe and welcome at home.

DIGITAL CONTAINER - The purpose of this website is to for further understanding of the Guide to Urban Planning mentioned above. It will consists of three main sections, one for understanding the problem through interactive diagrams, another as a Research & Data archive and the last as a section for people to get informed on what it is they can do to get involved.

STORYTELLING - This part will contain interactive diagrams that will clearly show the problem and visually help understand the struggles many undocumented folks go through.

RESEARCH & DATA - This section will contain all the research and data that was used to make the proposal of the Guide and further look into Sanctuary City Policy and my analysis. Basically how I came to the solutions I am proposing.

WHAT CAN YOU DO - This section is for individuals, or each stakeholder, to understand the power they have and how they are able to help the undocumented community. It serves to build awareness, get involved in local activist group and/or to work more holistically and plan and design for an inclusive, healthy and safe community, where all are welcome.
